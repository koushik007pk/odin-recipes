Pizza

Description

Pizza is a Italian dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough covered in tomato sauce and cheese, usually made from flour, water, and yeast. It is topped with various toppings, such as mozzarella, basil, olives, and various meats, vegetables, and sauces.

Pizza is often topped with a variety of cheeses, including mozzarella, gouda, parmesan, and cheddar. Other popular toppings include mushrooms, olives, onions, pineapple, ham, bell peppers, and spinach. The crust of the pizza is typically thin and crispy, with a smooth, golden crust that gives the pizza its distinctive Italian.
